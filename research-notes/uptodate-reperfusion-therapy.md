# UpToDate Research Notes: Reperfusion Therapy for Acute Ischemic Stroke
## Source: User-extracted facts from UpToDate

### Key Clinical Facts

**Alteplase (tPA)**
- IVT improves outcomes within 4.5h of onset
- 0-3h: NNT=10 for good outcome (mRS 0-1), 33% vs 23% control
- 3-4.5h: NNT=20, 35% vs 30% control
- Beyond 4.5h: no significant benefit
- sICH rate: 6.8% vs 1.3% control (NNH=18)
- Benefit similar regardless of age or stroke severity
- Every 15-min reduction in door-to-needle time: 4% better walking, 3% home discharge, 4% less mortality

**Tenecteplase**
- FDA approved Feb 2025 for acute ischemic stroke
- Single bolus: 0.25 mg/kg (max 25 mg) — easier than alteplase infusion
- Similar efficacy and safety to alteplase (2024 meta-analysis, 11 trials, n=7545)
- Faster door-to-needle times
- Higher doses (>=0.4 mg/kg) may be harmful (NOR-TEST 2 stopped early)

**Extended Window IVT (4.5-24h)**
- Imaging selection required (DWI-FLAIR mismatch or perfusion mismatch)
- HOPE trial: alteplase 4.5-24h with CTP selection, mRS 0-1: 40.3% vs 26.3%
- TWIST trial: noncontrast CT alone NOT sufficient for wake-up stroke selection
- 2025 meta-analysis: tenecteplase 4.5-24h with perfusion selection improved outcomes when EVT not available

**Endovascular Thrombectomy (EVT)**
- For LVO in anterior circulation (or basilar), up to 24h
- DAWN trial: 6-24h with clinical-core mismatch
- DEFUSE 3: 6-16h with perfusion mismatch
- Bridging (IVT + EVT) favored over EVT alone
- IRIS meta-analysis: IVT beneficial if started within 2h20min of onset

**In-Hospital Timeline Goals**
- Code stroke activation: immediately on EMS pre-notification
- CT/MRI initiation: <=20 min
- Head CT interpretation: <=35 min
- IVT start: <=45 min (door-to-needle)
- EVT start: <=90 min

**tPA Eligibility (Key Points)**
- Blood glucose is ONLY mandatory test before tPA
- Consent not required for otherwise eligible patient when surrogate not available
- Minor nondisabling stroke: DAPT may be as good as IVT (ARAMIS trial)
- PRISMS: IVT vs aspirin for minor nondisabling stroke — no difference (stopped early)

**Prourokinase**
- PROST-2 trial from China: similar efficacy to alteplase, possibly lower sICH

**Special Populations**
- Age >=80: benefit from IVT despite higher mortality
- Pregnancy: relative contraindication, can be given after careful discussion
- DOACs: relative contraindication, may be safe if normal coag tests or >48h since last dose
